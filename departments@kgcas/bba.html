BBA

Department of Management Education

Profile

Bachelor of Business Administration often termed as BBA is a three-year undergraduate program which covers all aspects of business. Apart from this, there are variety of specializations in various field such as finance, marketing, sales, human resource management, international business, information technology, etc.The degree aims to focus on teaching students the fundamentals of business and management principles through classroom lectures, case studies, industry visits and internships.

Career Opportunities

Sales Executive

Marketing Executive

Management Trainee

Market Research Analyst

Human Resourse Management

Business Development Executive

Further study

After completing BBA, one can also opt for higher education. Although there is no dearth of opportunities for BBA graduates, be it the government sector, public sector or private sector, there are many higher degree programmes available for students with a Bachelors degree in Business Administration like

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Master of Management Studies (MMS)

Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM)

Post Graduate Program in Management(PGPM)

PGD in Bank Management
