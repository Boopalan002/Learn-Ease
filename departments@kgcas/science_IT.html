B.Sc.IT

Department of Information Technology

Profile

The Department was started in the year 2008. The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (B.Sc. IT) is a 3-year (6 semesters) undergraduate program affiliated to Bharathiar University and Recognized by the Government of Tamilnadu. The course develops Information Technology professionals with strong fundamental knowledge, understanding of recent development tools used in IT industry and also creates foundation for future research. The program is designed for those who wish to obtain broad-based and leading-edge skills in Information Technology. Sufficient Text books, Reference books and Journals are provided to meet the studentsâ€™ requirements.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)
