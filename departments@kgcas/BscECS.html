B.Sc ECS

Department of Electronics and Communications

Profile

The Department of Electronics and Communication Systems was established in the year 2013. The department has 5 faculty members working for the benefit of the students.
Electronics and Communication opens great career opportunities for the students in manufacturing industries, telecommunication industries, research and development, service organizations like broadcasting, data communication, entertainment, system support etc.

Career Opportunities

Television Production Manager

Audiovisual Production Specialist

Radio Journalist

Communication System Operator

Broadcast and Sound Technician
