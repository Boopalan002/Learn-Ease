Department of Computer Science (PG)

Profile

The Department of Computer Science was established in 2006. M.Sc. Computer Science degree program was introduced in the year 2007 to admit the first batch of the M.Sc. Computer Science students.
The department has completed 14 years of noble service in the field of higher education. In the year 2021. M.Sc. Computer Science is introduced with the latest curriculum as per the requirements of the Software Industry.
